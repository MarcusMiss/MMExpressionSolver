## Overview

``MMExpressionSolver`` support various operators.

## Operators

``MMExpressionSolver`` supports mathematical and logical operators.

### Mathematical operators

- `+` : as infix and prefix operator
- `-` : as infix and prefix operator
- `*` : as infix operator
- `/` : as infix operator

### Logical operators

As infix operators:

- `&&`, `AND`
- `||`, `OR`
- `^`, `XOR`
- `==`, `=`
- `>=`, `=>`, `≥`
- `<=`, `=<`, `≤`
- `>`
- `<`
- `<>`, `!=`, `≠`

As prefix operators:

- `!`, `NOT`
